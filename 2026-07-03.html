Cross-cutting observations

  • The enterprise-governance layer is the frontier now. With frontier models paused in preview (GPT-5.6, Gemini 3.5 Pro) or freshly restored (Fable 5), the day's real shipping was operational: Anthropic's Enterprise analytics + spend alerts + model-level entitlements and the Claude apps gateway are about attributing cost and enforcing access for agents already in production.
